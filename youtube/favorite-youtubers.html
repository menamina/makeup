another reason i enjoy her videos is because she does a lot of affordable makeup reviews which as someone who typically buys drugstore products i love that. now i am willing to spend more money on something that i know i wont go through as quickly, if i've thought about it, and if it makes sense. for examplea blush or bronzer or eyeshadow pallet. i do not go through blush as quickly as i do concealer, setting spray, or lip products so to me spending $30+ on one blush or one bronzer is not going to break the bank. i definitely believe in beauty on a budget and just because a product is more expensive does not mean that 1: you're going to be better at doing makeup and 2: that it is any better than a drugstore product. to me makeup really comes down to technique, it does not matter if your product is $50 or $5. what matters is the quality of that product which can be it or miss irregardless of price and your application technique.

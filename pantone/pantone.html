Meaning + Symbolism

The color brown is usually associated with resilience, probably due to its earthy tones. It is viewed as a color that provides safety and security. It improves our lives by reminding us about the things that are most important, connections to earth, home, and family. Therefore, it inspires us to appreciate the small things in life.

Personality Type’s Associated With Brown

People with the brown personality type are down-to-earth and confident.

MBTI

ISTJ [Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging]: Brown is often linked to ISTJs due to their practical, responsible, and detail-oriented nature. They are known for their strong work ethic and commitment to fulfilling their obligations.

ESTJ [Extroverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging]: ESTJs, like ISTJs, are also associated with brown due to their practicality, organizational skills, and focus on efficiency. They are often seen as reliable leaders who value structure and tradition.

Enneagram

6w5: the Loyalist. They are typically described as loyal, responsible, and hard-working — frequently seeking reassurance and stability.

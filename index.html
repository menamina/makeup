About

These days I am not as deep into makeup as I once was when I was 16. And while I no longer know about the latest makeup releases or browse Ulta + Sephora at least once a week, I do still regularly wear and enjoy makeup.
I enjoy it so much that I created this blog dedicated to it, well.. cosmetics in general, with a focus on makeup!

Intentions

I am someone who enjoys an aesthetic physical environment such as my curated room, but also an aesthetically pleasing website.
The Ordinary, Seint's, and Pattern Beauty are just a few cosmetic sites I think are beautifully made.
